# Example: Topic -> Chapter Plan

## Prompt

```text
Use $fdu-final-paper-skill to design a Fudan master's thesis chapter plan.
Topic: 基于多源遥感数据的城市热岛效应时空演化研究
Degree: 学术型硕士
Evidence: Landsat 2013-2023, MODIS LST, POI density, administrative boundary data
Methods: LST inversion, spatial autocorrelation, geographically weighted regression
Submission batch: 2026-09
```

## Output Sketch

### Proposed Chapter Tree

1. 绪论
   - Purpose: define the urban heat island problem, research gap, objectives, and contribution boundaries.
   - Evidence: policy background, recent remote-sensing literature, study-area rationale.
2. 理论基础与数据方法
   - Purpose: define LST inversion, spatial autocorrelation, GWR, data preprocessing, and accuracy checks.
   - Evidence: dataset table, preprocessing workflow, variable dictionary.
3. 城市地表温度时空演化特征
   - Purpose: answer how heat island intensity changed from 2013 to 2023.
   - Evidence: LST maps, trend statistics, hot-spot migration.
4. 热岛效应影响因素及空间异质性
   - Purpose: explain how POI density, land cover, and urban form relate to heat patterns.
   - Evidence: Moran's I, GWR coefficients, robustness checks.
5. 热岛缓解分区与优化建议
   - Purpose: translate findings into planning zones and actionable strategies.
   - Evidence: risk zoning map, case comparison, limitation notes.
6. 结论与展望
   - Purpose: synthesize findings, contributions, limitations, and future work.
   - Evidence: conclusions tied to Chapters 3-5 only.
