# Golden Sketch: frontmatter-custom-sources

## Source Declaration

- Sources used: uploaded fixture front matter, pasted department note, and default Fudan 2026.06 baseline.
- The answer should state that official Graduate School and current department sources remain final authority, and that this is not an official approval decision.

## Core Output

- Readiness status should be "not ready to submit" or "needs revision before submission", not an official pass.
- Source hierarchy should prefer current Graduate School rules, then the pasted department note for international-student naming and school template requirements.
- The audit should flag passport-name mismatch, missing CLC/UDC, missing official template confirmation, missing declarations, missing final PDF inspection, figure/table list risk, and confidentiality approval unknowns.

## Claim-Evidence Risks

- Claim-evidence risks: do not assert compliance from extracted text alone.
- Evidence status should distinguish observed draft fields from missing files and unverified official sources.

## Missing Inputs

- Missing inputs: official school template, full front matter, final PDF/DOCX, passport record, originality statement, confidentiality status, figure/table list, and reference format sample.
- Do not invent missing approval records, passport details, template fields, or final PDF inspection results.

## Compliance Unknowns

- Compliance unknowns: department confirmation, Graduate School template version, secrecy procedure, GB/T 7714 transition, and whether the school has stricter front matter wording.

## Behavior Checks Covered

- declare sources
- list unknowns
- bound claims to evidence
- avoid unverified official conclusions
- avoid fabricated data

## Expected Checks Covered

- custom source hierarchy
- not ready to submit
- passport-name mismatch
- missing official template
- confidentiality approval unknown
