# Golden Sketch: conclusion-overclaim

## Source Declaration

- Sources used: prompt only; no conclusion draft, district dataset, methods, or result tables supplied.
- Compliance baseline: Fudan 2026.06 requires conclusions in the main text, but exact department conclusion format is unverified.

## Core Output

- Bounded conclusion should narrow nationwide policy value to one district and one year.
- Specific limitations should name geography, time span, data source, model assumptions, and external validity.
- Future work tied to limits should propose multi-year, multi-district, or validation expansion rather than unrelated projects.

## Claim-Evidence Risks

- Claim-evidence risks: nationwide value, general policy transfer, and long-term effect claims are unsupported.
- Keep only findings directly supported by the supplied district-year evidence.

## Missing Inputs

- Missing inputs: original conclusion, district name, year, methods, result tables, comparison cases, and validated policy recommendations.

## Compliance Unknowns

- Compliance unknowns: department preference for "主要工作/主要结论/创新点/不足与展望" subsectioning and research-output placement.
- Confirm final chapter naming against current template.

## Expected Checks Covered

- bounded conclusion
- specific limitations
- future work tied to limits
- no new evidence
