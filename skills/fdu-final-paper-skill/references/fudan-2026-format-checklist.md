# Fudan 2026 Thesis Specification Checklist

Use this reference for Fudan University master's and doctoral thesis formatting, front matter, references, notes, and submission-readiness checks.

## Authority and Effective Date

- Official page: https://gs.fudan.edu.cn/6b/9f/c2806a27551/page.htm
- Official list page: https://gs.fudan.edu.cn/2806/list.htm
- Current official file on the page: `复旦大学博士、硕士学位论文规范（2026.06修订版）.doc`
- The official page was published on 2026-06-15.
- User-provided Graduate School notice states the revised specification applies from September 2026; use it for the September 2026 degree batch and later unless a newer rule supersedes it.
- GB/T 7714-2025 `信息与文献 参考文献著录规则` takes effect on 2026-07-01. For submissions near the transition, verify whether the school, department, or library requires GB/T 7714-2025 or an older format.

## Cover and Title Page

Check the current official template, not old local examples.

- Treat the cover as the title page.
- Include Chinese Library Classification (CLC/中图分类号) and UDC classification when required by the 2026 template.
- For international students and foreign teachers, ensure names match passport English names.
- Use the revised field names and field positions from the current template.
- Do not use old public confidential-thesis cover samples. For confidential theses, contact the Graduate School Degree Office for the standard confidential cover sample.
- For confidential thesis applications, confirm the required timing and process before thesis proposal/opening. If the opening has already happened, do not assume the application can still be accepted.
- Check revised spine content against the current template.

## Content Arrangement

- Verify font rules for English letters and numbers.
- Verify Chinese abstract, English abstract, and keyword requirements in the current rule.
- For theses written in a non-Chinese language, check the revised Chinese abstract length requirement against the 2026 specification.
- Add a list of figures and a list of tables when figures/tables exist and the rule says they are required.
- Ensure the main text includes conclusions, either as a final chapter or as a clearly marked conclusion section.
- Format references according to the applicable GB/T 7714 rule; verify the transition to GB/T 7714-2025 for submissions after 2026-07-01.
- Place notes according to the revised requirement; do not assume prior footnote/endnote placement rules still apply.

## Compliance Audit Steps

1. Identify the degree batch and department.
2. Download or open the current official template/specification.
3. Compare front matter field by field.
4. Check abstract/keywords, contents, figure/table lists, abbreviations, references, notes, appendices, research outputs, and acknowledgements.
5. Check reference style and citation-bibliography consistency.
6. Check that every figure/table is cited in text and has a clear caption.
7. Check the final PDF/DOCX after conversion, not only the source file.
8. Record unknowns as questions for the department or Degree Office.
